About Me

I am interested in complex analysis, complex geometry, and partial differential equations. I received my Ph.D. degree from Purdue University under the supervision of László Lempert. I was a postdoc at Academia Sinica and then a visiting assistant professor at National Tsing Hua University. From August to December 2025, I will be a postdoc at the Erdős Center in Budapest. I am currently on the job market.
